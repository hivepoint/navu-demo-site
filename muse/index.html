Your Content Brief

Target Content Score. Objectively measure content comprehensiveness and know that your content is the best on any topic.
Subheading Suggestions. Structure your content to ensure you include the most important subsections.
Questions to Answer. Learn your audience’s burning questions so you can give the best answers.
Related Topics. Analyze tens of thousands of pages on your topic in minutes so you can write like an expert.
Internal Linking. Connect your content to other relevant pages on your domain and build topic clusters.
External Linking. Link to authoritative, but non-competitive sources to establish your credibility.

How it works

  1. We take your direction on a topic or keyword to create or optimize a piece of content around.
  2. You assign an overall layout to the document by outlining your subheading structure.
  3. Our proprietary topic modeling technology analyzes tens of thousands of pages on your topic and all of the subheadings in just minutes.
  4. You can edit our suggestions, hide things that you don’t like, add comments and get it prepped for writing.
  5. Use the shareable link in your file to get a writer in with no account.
  6. Your writer does their work directly in the Brief, getting scored against the suggestions as they go!
